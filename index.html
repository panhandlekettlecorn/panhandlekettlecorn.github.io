About Us

Panhandle Kettle Corn is a new business serving the panhandle of Nebraska. We offer several delicious kettle corn products at events around the panhandle. We also partner with businesses, schools, and other organization or fundraisers and promotional events.

Community Events

Look for the bright red Panhandle Kettle Corn tent at community events, fairs and festivals around the panhandle. We are cooking up fresh batches of our Old Fashioned Sweet & Salty Kettle Corn at every event. You'll smell it before you see us!

We also serve these other delicious flavors (selection varies per event):

  • Buttered Popcorn
  • Caramel Corn
  • White Cheddar
  • Ranch
  • Rainbow Splash
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Cinnamon Kettle Corn

We offer 3 convenient sizes:

  • Eat it Now (small) -- $3.00
  • Share with Others, Or Not! (medium) -- $5.00
  • Take Some Home (large) -- $8.00

Promotional Events

Bring the fun to your organization's next promotional or customer appreciation event. Panhandle Popcorn will set-up and serve fresh Kettle Corn and other delicious popcorn flavors.

  • Minimum Fee is $225.00 (6-8 hr day)
  • Includes 150 bags of kettle corn (10")
  • Extra bags over 150 are $1.50 each

Fundraisers & School Events

Include Panhandle Kettle Corn in your next fundraiser or school event and generate extra revenue. For school events, we offer our Old Fashioned Sweet & Salty Kettle Corn in the school colors. Proceeds from sales are donated to a designated cause or school program.

Donations from each bag:

  • $1.00 per large bag
  • $0.75 per medium bag
  • $0.50 per small bag
